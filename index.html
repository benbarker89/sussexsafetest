About Sussex Safe Test

Sussex Safe Test provides comprehensive PAT (Portable Appliance Testing) services across Sussex and surrounding areas. We are committed to ensuring your electrical appliances are safe, compliant, and meet all current regulations.

Our qualified engineers use the latest testing equipment to provide thorough inspections and certifications for all types of portable electrical equipment. We serve both commercial and residential clients with professional, reliable service.
